Stakeholder Q&A

Key insights for the POC report to address common concerns.

Q1: Can a Business Basic user open an attachment with a sensitivity label sent by a Business Standard user?

Answer: Yes. The Business Basic user will be able to decrypt and open the document, provided they authenticate with a valid Microsoft 365 identity. The security is linked to the user's identity, not the specific license tier.

Q2: How does the applied sensitivity label affect access?

Answer: The sensitivity label functions as an "identity gatekeeper". Once applied, it enforces the restrictions defined in the label's access control settings (e.g., "No Print" or "No Forward") consistently on the recipient's device. This ensures that even users on different license tiers (Business Standard vs. Business Basic) are bound by the same Orysys security standards.
